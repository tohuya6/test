\subsection{Resistors in Series and Parallel}
Resistors control current flow in circuits, and their arrangement affects total resistance.
\begin{itemize}
    \item \textbf{Series Resistors:} Connected end-to-end, current is the same through each, and total resistance adds up.
    \begin{equation}
        R_{\text{eq}} = R_1 + R_2 + R_3 + \cdots
    \end{equation}
    \item \textbf{Parallel Resistors:} Connected across the same voltage, total conductance is the reciprocal sum.
    \begin{equation}
        \frac{1}{R_{\text{eq}}} = \frac{1}{R_1} + \frac{1}{R_2} + \frac{1}{R_3} + \cdots
    \end{equation}
\end{itemize}
